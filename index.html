How it works

Enjoy your favorite dumplings in 3 simple steps

1

Select what you like and order

Ordering Rumbling Dumpling has never been easier: Just click on 'Order Now' button on any device of your preference, choose your favorite dumpling type and desired delivery time, and leave the rest to us!

2

We make it and deliver to your door

Upon receiving your order, our kitchen will prepare your dumplings with fresh organic ingredients. We will then deliver the ready-made dumplings to your door at your convenience.

3

Receive, Fast-prep, and Enjoy!

Upon receiving the package, just follow our award-winning Fast-prep© recipe to prep and enjoy Rumbling Dumpling! If you are short on time, just freeze up the package for later use, it'll stay fresh for weeks in your freezer.
